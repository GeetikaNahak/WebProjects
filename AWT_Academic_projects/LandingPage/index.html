Welcome to VIIT

Vignan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was established in 2002 in the City of Destiny, Visakhapatnam, to cater to the educational needs of the general public. It acquired Autonomous status in the year 2017 and is an NBA and NAAC(A Grade) accredited Institute.
