Student Academic Info

Courses Offered: We offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines such as Computer Science, Engineering, Management, and Humanities.

Grading System: The academic performance of students is evaluated through a CGPA system. Each student is provided with regular feedback on their academic progress through grade cards.

Attendance: Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in every course to be eligible for examinations.

Training and Placement Details

Placement Cell: Our institution has a dedicated Placement Cell that works with industry leaders to provide job opportunities to students. Regular training sessions are conducted to enhance the employability skills of students.

Internships: Students undergo internships with reputed companies in their respective fields, gaining hands-on experience and industry exposure.

Recruiters: Major companies such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Accenture are among our top recruiters, providing placement opportunities in both technical and managerial roles.

Student Chapters

GFG Student Chapter: The GFG Chapter conducts workshops, conferences, and technical paper presentations, giving students a platform to improve their technical skills.

ACM Student Chapter: The ACM Chapter encourages students to participate in coding competitions, hackathons, and project showcases, promoting innovation and technical prowess.

GDG: Students organize and participate in coding competitions, hackathons, technical fests, promoting teamwork, leadership, and creativity.
